Our Story

Brew & Beans started as a simple idea: create a welcoming space where people could connect over great coffee and good conversation. We source small-batch beans from trusted roasters, roast in small batches, and craft drinks with care. Our shop is a place to pause, savor, and linger a little longer.

Every cup tells a story—from the farmers who tended the beans to the baristas who greet you with a smile. We’re grateful to be part of a neighborhood that values quality, community, and sustainability.
